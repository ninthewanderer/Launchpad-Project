--------- [ INSTRUCTIONS ] ---------

This is the platforming level for our game. As the player, your goal is to
reach the platforms in the sky and rescue your cat. 

Follow the lights and use your movement abilities wisely!


--------- [ CONTROLS ] ---------

W - Move Forward
A - Move Left
S - Move Backward
D - Move Right
Press Space - Jump
Hold Space - Vertical Boost Ability
Press Shift - Dash
Hold Left Control - Sprint
Escape Key - Pause Game


--------- [ KEY MECHANICS ] ---------

This level grants the player access to Rocket Boots, which is a vertical boost ability that allows the player to jump higher.

Additionally, there are chimneys in the environment that when jumped on will propel the player upwards without consuming their movement ability, allowing them to reach greater heights.

Interact with the floating hearts at certain checkpoints to restore 1 life.
